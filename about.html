Our Mission

Turning Ambition into IT Careers

LearnMore Academy was founded with one mission: to make quality IT education accessible, practical, and job-focused for everyone in India — regardless of their educational background or prior technical knowledge.

We believe that the gap between where students are and where the IT industry needs them to be is a problem we can solve — with the right curriculum, the right trainers, and genuine career support.
